10.1 Supply

In Stem, the biomass supply only sources from retranslocation (Fig. 10.2). Daily retranslocation is the proportion of current storage (\(W_{stem, storage}\)). The default value of proportion is 0.5 since StartGrainFill, i.e. retranslocatable biomsss is 50% during grain filling (Fig. 10.1).
